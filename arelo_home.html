How it works

One place for
everything you need to do

Arelo finds the tasks hiding in your inbox, takes anything else you throw at it, and gives you one calm list.

01
🔗
Connect your inbox

Securely link your Gmail or Outlook. Arelo reads your recent emails to understand your patterns — who matters, what's urgent, when you're quiet — and pulls out every action hiding in your threads.

02
🧠
Add anything else

Not everything lives in email. Use Arelo's "Unload" feature to dump tasks from your head — work projects, home admin, school forms, things you're waiting on — and Arelo will sort and prioritise them too.

03
✅
Act, don't search

Your dashboard shows exactly what needs doing across every area of your life — Work, Home, People, Admin — prioritised, explained, and calm. One thing at a time.
