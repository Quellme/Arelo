What Qwell Me does

Your inbox is not the problem.
The sorting is.

Most tools show you everything and expect you to decide what matters. Qwell Me makes that decision — so you don't have to.

📧

Reads everything

Emails, notifications, school updates, billing reminders — Qwell Me reviews it all before you have to.

🎯

Decides what matters

Not a filter. Not a summary. Qwell Me tells you what actually needs your attention today — and why.

🗂️

Holds everything else

Things that can wait are parked safely — not deleted, not buried. They surface when they need to.

🔁

Tracks follow-ups

When something isn't done in one go, you add a note and a reminder. Qwell Me keeps the thread alive.

How it works

Three steps to a calmer day.

Qwell Me does the hard part before you even open it.

01

Connect your accounts

Link your inbox, calendar, and any other sources you want Qwell Me to watch. Securely — it reads, never sends.

02

Qwell Me reviews everything

Every email, update, and notification is read, assessed, and sorted. Urgency, effort, and consequence are all weighed up.

03

You get a short list

Open Qwell Me and see just the things that need you today — with a clear next step for each one. Nothing else in the way.
