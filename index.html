How it works

Your PA, not a filter

Qwell Me doesn't just sort your email. It acts like a personal assistant who knows what you need and when.

1

Connect your inbox

Link your Gmail account in two clicks. Qwell Me reads your last 90 days to understand your email habits and what matters to you.

2

Your AI learns you

It studies how you treat different senders, what you act on, and what you ignore — building a picture unique to you.

3

Morning & evening bulletins

You get two clear briefings a day. What needs doing, what can wait, what you can ignore entirely.

4

Confirm, don't react

Nothing happens automatically. Your PA recommends; you decide. One tap to confirm, defer, or dismiss.

Privacy

Your data is yours

We take this seriously — especially because your inbox is personal.

🔐 Read-only access

Qwell Me reads your email but never sends, modifies, or deletes anything without your explicit confirmation.

🚫 Never sold

Your email data is never sold, shared, or used to train models outside your own personalisation.

🗑️ Delete anytime

Disconnect your account and all your data is deleted within 24 hours. No questions asked.

🔒 Encrypted

All email content is encrypted in transit and at rest. We only store what's needed to run your bulletins.
